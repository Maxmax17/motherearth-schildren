SMOKE BELCHING

Smoke belching is usually the bad smoke coming from cars
smoke is described as "belching" from industrial smoke stacks as a reference to human belching of gas from the stomach.The word is originally imitative of the sound of human belching.Essentially it is a rush of gas from a pipe.

Definition Of Terms

SMOKE-The product of combustion,consisting of fine particles of carbon carried by hot gases and air

BELCHING-a violent emittance of flame,smoke,gas etc.

Solution Summary

Through the use of our website, we will explain the bad effects and the reasons why we should stop smoke belching by making 5mins. video and to let everyone know that the smoke belching is a bad habit.Then we´ll post it on social media to spread it easily.

Background Of The Problem

Our problem is smoke belching, we focused on this problems because we are encountering it everyday, also smoke belching is one of the big cause of air pollution and we came up with this solution because a lot of people can see it and it can spread easily because of the social media.

Causes Of The Problem

The causes of the problem which is smoke belching is lack of knowledge about how to take care of cars,do not observe proper driving habits and they drive at fast.Therefore they produce to much smoke that can cause air pollution.

How The Solution Works

The solution works by,using internet connection and by using gadgets that will allow us to go in our website.

Features Of The Solution

It show us what is smoke belching, what are the cause and effects of it and how to avoid smoke belching.

Benefits Of The Solution

Our website will create awareness on smoke belching, what it is, what are the bad effects and how we can prevent it. Our reader´s will encouraged to maintain their cars to prevent smoke belching.
